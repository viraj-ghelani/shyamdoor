About Us

Crafting Doors That Define Spaces

Shyam Door is a trusted name in the door manufacturing industry, specializing in high-quality lamination doors and door skins that combine aesthetics with durability.

Our products are crafted using MR Grade Wood (Pine & Commercial), with precision lamination technology ensuring long-lasting finish and beautiful design. We offer an extensive range of designs to suit every architectural style — from contemporary to traditional.

Flush Door MR Grade Wood (Pine & Commercial)
Thickness: 30mm, 35mm & 40mm
50 Micron Chemical Coating
Marble Frame & Wood Frame Border Options
Glossy, Matt & Texture Finish Available
